## DIGITAL-LITERACY-PROJECT

## Overview
This project focused on Digital Literacy Awareness, highlighting the importance of using the digital technologies safely, effectively, and responsibly. It cover the practical skills, online platforms, cybersecurity awareness, and professional communications need for students in today’s digital world.

## Objectives
* Understand the concept of digital literacy
* Promote safe internet practices
* Build professional digital profile
* Enhance technical and communication skills
* Spread awareness about cyber fraud and preventions 

## Project Content

1. Digital Literacy Awareness
* Explanation of digital literacy concepts
* Importance of safe and responsible internet usage
* Awareness of digital tools and platforms 

2. Student Digital Profiles
* Creation and use of platforms like:
    .GitHub (projects & coding)
    .LinkedIn (professional networking)
    .Kaggle (data science learning)
    .HackerRank (coding practice)
* Help in skills development and career growth 

3. Quiz & Skill Development
* Digital literacy quiz using Google Forms
* Data analysis using Google Sheets
* Coding practices through HackerRank
* Combines theoretical and practical learning 

4. Professional Communication
* Formal email writing:
    .Assignment extension request
    .Internship application
* Social media checklists:
    .Do’s and Dont's
    .Online presence management
    .Digital responsibility 

5. UPI Fraud & Prevention
* Case study on UPI fraud in India
* Common scams techniques
* Prevention tips:
    .Do not share OTP or UPI PIN
    .Avoid suspicious links
    .Enable two factor authentication
* Reporting channels:
    .National Cyber Crime Portal
    .Helpline: 1930 


## Tools & Technologies Used

* Google Forms & Sheets
* GitHub
* LinkedIn
* Kaggle
* HackerRank

## Key Learnings

* Importance of digital safety
* Building an online professional identity
* Practical coding and data handling skills
* Awareness of cyber threats and prevention

## Conclusion
Digital literacy is essential for students to succeed in academics and career. This project highlight how technical knowledge, safe practices, and professional communications together help to become a responsible digital citizen.
