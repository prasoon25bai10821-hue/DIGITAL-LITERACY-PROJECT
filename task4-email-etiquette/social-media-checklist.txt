Social Media Do's ✅

Update your LinkedIn: Ensure your education section reflects your current degree and institution (VIT Bhopal).

Showcase your projects: Share links to your GitHub repositories or Kaggle notebooks to build a professional brand.


Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Always secure your accounts to prevent unauthorized access and identity theft.


Network with alumni: Connect with seniors and alumni in your branch to learn about internship and placement opportunities.


Verify information: Before sharing a post or news, check the source to prevent the spread of misinformation.






Social Media Don'ts ❌

Avoid online arguments: Engaging in toxic debates or using offensive language can damage your professional reputation.


Don't share sensitive data: Never post your registration number, password, or financial details (like UPI IDs) publicly.

Stop "Doom-scrolling": Manage your time effectively; don't let social media interfere with your coding practice or GATE preparation.


Don't ignore privacy settings: Avoid keeping your personal profiles entirely public to strangers.


Avoid unprofessional photos: Ensure that your profile pictures on professional platforms like LinkedIn or GitHub remain formal.
