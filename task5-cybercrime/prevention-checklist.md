📄 Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

* Use strong, unique passwords for each accounts and enable two factor authentication (2FA) wherever it possible.
* Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone even if they claim that they are from a bank or company.
* Always verify links before clicking; avoid suspicious emails, messages, or unknown attachments (common publishing methods).
* UPI Safety Tip: Do not approve collect requests unless you are sure you enter PIN only to send money, not to receive it.
* UPI Safety Tip: Avoid installing unknown apps or screen sharing apps suggested by strangers during calls.
* Use only official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm downloaded from trusted app stores(like playstore,appstore).
* Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against security risks.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
* Log out from shared or public computers after use.
* Regularly check bank statements and transaction history for any suspicious activities.
